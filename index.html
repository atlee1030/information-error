Error of Information
Project 1 Project 2

Thanks to the development of the internet, we can now easily share and access vast amounts of information.

However, because the exchange of information has become so easy, it has become difficult to convey accurate information due to the prevalence of fake or misleading information. Furthermore, information overload causes people's interests to shift rapidly.

The social issues you enter on this website will be haphazardly linked to other information within the site and will quickly disappear. This is not merely a webpage designed to explore other people's interests, but rather a representation of the current flaws and realities of information dissemination.
